About Me

I am a dedicated and goal-oriented Nigerian academic professional with a strong background in technical and vocational education. I hail from Nsukka in Enugu State, Nigeria, where I was also born and raised. My educational journey and professional experiences reflect my commitment to continuous learning, teaching excellence, and the development of practical skills in education.

My formal education began in Nsukka, Enugu State, where I completed my secondary education at Nsukka High School. I successfully obtained my Senior Secondary Certificate Examination (SSCE) in 2013. Driven by a passion for technical and applied sciences, I proceeded to Our Saviour Institute of Science and Agricultural Technology, where I earned my Ordinary National Diploma (OND). This phase of my education provided me with a solid foundation in scientific and technical principles. In pursuit of higher qualifications, I advanced to Institute of Management and Technology Enugu, where I obtained my Higher National Diploma (HND) in 2018. My time at IMT further strengthened my technical knowledge and professional competence.

Why I am Pursuing Web Development! With web development, I’m not limited to just one job path. I can freelance, work remotely, or even build my own tech projects or business. That flexibility really matters to me because I want control over my career and income. Currently, I am undertaking a Postgraduate Diploma in Industrial Technical Education at the University of Nigeria Nsukka, within the Faculty of Vocational and Technical Education. This program is equipping me with advanced pedagogical skills and deepening my expertise in technical education and training.
